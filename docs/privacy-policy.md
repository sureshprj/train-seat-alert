# Train Seat Alert Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-06-14

Train Seat Alert is an independent mobile app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Indian Railways, IRCTC, or CRIS.

## Information Stored On The Device

The app stores trip details, travel dates, alert settings, notification history, booking reminder state, cached train and station reference data, and rail session cookies locally on the user's device using SQLite/app storage.

## Information Sent To Third Parties

When a user searches for trains or stations, loads a CAPTCHA, or checks seat availability, the app sends the relevant train, station, date, class, quota, CAPTCHA, and session-cookie information to Indian Rail public web services.

The Android app displays Google AdMob banner ads. Google and its advertising partners may process device, app, advertising, diagnostics, and approximate location data according to Google's services and the user's device settings.

## Notifications

The app uses local/native notifications for availability alerts, booking-window reminders, and CAPTCHA-required reminders. Notification content may include trip names and availability summaries.

## Data Sharing

The app developer does not operate a backend server for this app and does not directly sell user trip data. Third-party services used by the app, including Indian Rail services and Google AdMob, process data under their own terms and privacy practices.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete trips and clear notifications inside the app. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data from the device according to Android system behavior.
